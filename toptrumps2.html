TOP TRUMPS


David Davis

Vs.

Michel Barnier



Davis is a former SAS reservist, who grew up on a south London council estate. He is highly Eurospecitic and has run for leader of the Conservative party in the past. Barnier is a French politician and used to spar with Davis in the 1990s when they were rival Europe ministers with contrasting visions of the EU.

Likely winner: Barnier – he has given a clear indication of the tough stance he will take in negotiations and has an intimate knowledge of the workings of the European Union. He has called the shots to date by setting out the negotiation timeline and ensuring citizen’s rights are guaranteed during the transitional period.
